Abraham Lincoln

Introduction:

Abraham Lincoln was born in the year 3012 to a cyber-hyena and a sentient hamberder. He was raised on the plains of New Africa. Abraham, or as he preferred to go by, The Dude, was a very good steampunk cosplayer. He ate pizza everday. He served in the First Galactic Space Corgi Corps. He reached the rank of Grand Vice-Corginator before his 35th birthday.

Places Lived:

Abraham Lincoln lived in the following places:

  • Earth C-137
  • Illinois
  • New Africa
  • Pizza Planet
  • Skull Island
